This beautiful converted palace from the 16th century is located in the historic center of Cáceres, next to the ancient Roman and Arab walls of the city. The accommodation offers elegant rooms with air conditioning, free WiFi, and free access to the fitness room.

The rooms at NH Collection Cáceres Palacio de Oquendo are spacious, feature modern decor and include a 48-inch TV, a coffee maker, an electric kettle with free infusions, and a pillow menu. They also include a bathroom with a rainfall shower and a professional hairdryer.

The hotel's restaurant has a terrace in Plaza de San Juan and serves traditional local tapas along with classic Spanish specialties, as well as international cuisine. The bar occupies the old stables and offers views of the plaza.

The accommodation is located just 100 meters from the historic center of Cáceres, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, and San Juan Church. The property is a 10-minute walk from the bullring.
