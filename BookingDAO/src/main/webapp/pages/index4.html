This beautiful converted 16th-century palace is located in Cáceres' historic center, next to the city's ancient Roman and Arab walls. The property offers elegant rooms with air conditioning, free WiFi, and free access to the fitness room.

The rooms at NH Collection Cáceres Palacio de Oquendo are spacious, feature modern décor, and include a 48-inch TV, coffee machine, electric kettle with free tea, and a pillow menu. They also come with a bathroom that has a rain shower and a professional hairdryer.

The hotel's restaurant has a terrace in San Juan Square and serves traditional local tapas, classic Spanish dishes, and international cuisine. The bar is located in the former stables and offers views of the square.

The property is just 100 meters from Cáceres' historic center, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, and San Juan Church. It is also a 10-minute walk from the bullring.

Couples love the location — they rated it 9.6 for a two-person trip.
